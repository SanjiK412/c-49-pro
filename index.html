Fun facts about Camels

  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
  • Camels can travel up to 40 miles per hour- the same as a racehorse!
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms

Fun facts about Chameleons

  • Almost half of all known species of chamleons live in Madagascar
  • Chameleons mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature
  • Unlike many lizards, Chameleons can't regrow their tail

Fun facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Fun facts about Teddy Bears

  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906
  • Teddy Bear's were invented in honor of President Theodore Roosevelt
  • They're the best-traveled toys ever

Fun facts about Pandas

  • Panda's have great camaflouge for their environment
  • Panda's spend a lot of their day eating

Fun facts about Flying Cicada's

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
  • Most have red-orange eyes
  • They prune mature trees and erate the soil
